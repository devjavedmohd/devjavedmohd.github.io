Franchise Requirements:

To become Veer International Academy computer + english speaking mission franchisee, a person should have:

  • Minimum 100-sq. ft. Space.
  • Well profile and having capacity for social work.
  • Good accommodation and reachable location.
  • Ability to invest up to Rs. 200000
Franchise Support:
  • Branch Start-up support
  • Field assistance
  • Support in recruitment of center manager, faculty members, counsellors etc.
  • Marketing support
  • Technical support
  • Advertisement assistance: Advertisement cost in local/ regional newspaper is shared equally among all franchisee.
Franchise Benefits:
  • Excellent returns on investment
  • Promotional material like brochures, prospectus etc. will be supplied at production cost by VEER INTERNATIONAL ACADEMY COMPUTER+ENGLISH SPEAKING MISSION (Head Office) on requisition by the franchisee.

  • As a franchisee, you can get detailed operating manuals
  • Field assistance is available for franchisee
