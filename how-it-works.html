2. How will I be Paid?

We will add Store Credits to your GamesAdda.in account or transfer Cash through NEFT to your bank account, depending on the payment method you select for your sell order. The amount will be credited to you only after we receive your shipment and test the game(s) for quality.

It is your responsibility to make sure your shipment reaches us undamaged. We reserve the right to modify the item conditions in your sell order and pay you accordingly. See our Trade-in Policy for more details.

3. How Free Shipping, Free Pick Up, and Cash on Delivery Work?

  • We offer the only ‘no strings attached’ Free Shipping in India. All products sold on GamesAdda.in, no matter the price or weight, are shipped for free anywhere in India.
  • We offer free pick up to our sellers in 3000+ pin codes in India. For sellers from rest of the locations, we add Rs. 75 as shipping credits to your account to cover the shipping charges borne upfront by you.
  • We offer Cash on Delivery service in 2000+ locations in India, covering almost all the major cities. Cash on Delivery is offered only on orders of Rs.300 or more.
  1. We currently allow you to sell only Xbox 360, PS3, PSP and PSVITA games.
  2. We do not offer cash for the all the games that you can sell on GamesAdda.in website. You can check individual games (titles) to see whether we have a cash offering for it or not by click on the ‘Get price estimate’ link on the product page. Availability of a cash offering for any game usually depends on the popularity of the title.
  3. We currently offer free Pick Up in about 3000 PIN codes in India and as such may not be able to offer it at your particular location.
